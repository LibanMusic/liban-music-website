About Liban

Liban is a London‑based singer and songwriter whose music blends soulful vocals with heartfelt storytelling. Drawing inspiration from diverse genres and personal experiences, his songs take listeners on an emotional journey through love, hope and introspection. In 2025 he began sharing his work on SoundCloud, where he released a series of tracks including “upstage”, “That You Were There”, “Summer Girl”, “Twilight”, “without you”, “Before” and “me and you”【611341356011899†L17-L45】. Each release showcases Liban’s evolving artistry and his dedication to creating music that resonates with audiences worldwide. Beyond the studio, Liban continues to develop his craft and connect with fans through online platforms.
